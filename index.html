Dan's Bandanna Project

Dan's Bandanna Project was brought to Virginia Tech in 2018 to raise awareness for those who struggle with anxiety or stress. By displaying a white bandanna on your backpack, you are showing your support to those affected and in need of guidance or just a friendly face.
